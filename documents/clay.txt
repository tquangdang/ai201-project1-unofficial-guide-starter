Mitchel Clay is awesome and you will enjoy his classes. He could teach a baby how a processor works. Taking comp org with Clay will also teach you about half of the material needed for Operating Systems which is required afterwards.

Course CS3223 (difficulty 3/5, grade A):
Mr. Clay described computer architecture in a way that was easy to understand. His use of analogies and examples makes the complex concepts of computer architecture more digestible and easily graspable. I must admit that he can be a little lax at times, but he is an excellent instructor and I look forward to taking more of his class in the future!
